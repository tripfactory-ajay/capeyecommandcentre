Why CapeEye Was Built
The 6 problems every
growing dealership faces
These aren't unique — but without the right system they compound as your team and stock levels grow. CapeEye solves all six.
📱
WhatsApp Handovers
Stage completions buried in group chats. No record, no accountability, nothing to refer back to when something goes wrong.
Fixed — every handover logged permanently
📊
Spreadsheet Chaos
Multiple versions, always out of date. No one trusts the numbers so everyone makes their own copy and the problem multiplies.
Fixed — one live system, always current
❓
Where Is That Car?
No way to know which stage a vehicle is at, who has it, how long it's been there, or what's been done to it so far.
Fixed — 12-stage pipeline, live tracking
🔑
Lost Keys Every Day
No record of who took a key or when. Staff walk the yard asking before every test drive — wasting time and losing sales.
Fixed — 298 keys tracked via eTrack
⏰
Aged Stock Ignored
Vehicles sit 90+ days without anyone noticing. By the time it's flagged the margin is gone and remarketing costs more than the profit.
Fixed — automatic alerts at 60+ days
🚫
No Accountability
Disputes with no paper trail. Can't say who signed off a vehicle, when a stage was done, or what condition it was in.
Fixed — full audit trail, every action logged

📝 Stage Forms & Checklists
Each stage has its own form — condition notes, mechanic assigned, parts replaced, valet checklist, advertising channels. Nothing left to memory or message threads.
🔄 Smart Handover Popup
Completing a stage opens a popup showing the full history of previous stages and exactly what the next stage involves — context before every confirmation.
🎯 Priority & Accountability
Set Normal, Urgent or Critical on every handover. Every action is permanently logged in the Audit Trail with person, department and timestamp.
